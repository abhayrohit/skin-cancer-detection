Dataset Details
Title: Skin Cancer: Malignant vs. Benign

Description:
This dataset contains processed images of benign and malignant skin moles, providing a balanced collection of 1800 images for each category. The images are sourced from the ISIC Archive and have been resized to 224x224 pixels. The dataset aims to support machine learning models in predicting skin cancer types with the potential for better and more cost-effective diagnosis than dermatologists.

Source: ISIC Archive
Download Link: https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/fanconic/skin-cancer-malignant-vs-benign

Content Overview:

Training Data:
Benign: 1440 images
Malignant: 1197 images

Test Data:
Benign: 360 images
Malignant: 300 images

Usage:
This dataset is ideal for building machine learning models for binary classification of skin cancer.
